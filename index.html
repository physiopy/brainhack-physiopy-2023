Registration for Brainhack Physiopy 2023 is now open!

Brainhack Physiopy 2023 is an official satellite event of Brainhack Global 2023. The goal of the codesprint is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects, with a focus on non-neural physiology in neuroimaging.

Dates: December 4th - 5th, 2023

Costs: Free admission!

Location: Virtual hack, platform to be confirmed!

Participants new to Physiopy are encouraged to connect with us at brainhack.physiopy@gmail.com.
