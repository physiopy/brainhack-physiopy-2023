Registration for the Physiopy Spring 2023 Codesprint is now open!

The goal of the codesprint is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects, with a focus on non-neural physiology in neuroimaging.

Dates: April 13 - 14 2023

Costs: Free admission!

Location: Gathertown - Physiopy

Participants are encouraged to email project ideas to brainhack.physiopy@gmail.com.
